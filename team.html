Indian cricket team

About

  • The Indian cricket team is the team that represents India in the cricket game.
  • The Indian cricket team is a full member of the International Cricket Council (ICC).
  • Indian cricket team plays all formats i.e. Tests, One Day and T20 internationals.
  • Rohit Sharma is the captain and Virat Kohli is the vice-captain of the cricket team.
  • On 25th June 1932, the Indian cricket team was granted the status of test playing nation.
  • 13th July 1974, the Indian cricket team was granted the status of one day playing nation.
  • On 1st December 2006, the Indian cricket team played its maiden T20 against South Africa.
  • From 1935 to 1952, the Indian cricket team waited for 20 years to win a single test match.
  • The domestic cricket tournament of an Indian cricket team is known as ‘Ranji Trophy’.
  • Another domestic cricket tournament of an Indian cricket team is the ‘Duleep Trophy’.
